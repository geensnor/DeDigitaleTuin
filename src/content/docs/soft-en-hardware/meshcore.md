## Radio instellingen die werken

- Selected preset: EU/UK (Narrow), Switserland
- Frequency: 869.618
- Bandwith: 62.5 kHz
- Spreading Factor: 8
- Coding Rate: 8
- Transmit Power (dBm): 22
